Advocates Seek Clemency for Michigan Inmate Sickened by Prison Mold

Source: www.google.com 2026-04-17 2

Advocates are calling for clemency on behalf of a Michigan inmate who developed serious health complications following documented mold exposure at the state's sole women's correctional facility. At a press conference, speakers presented what they described as a growing body of allegations pointing to hazardous environmental conditions within the institution. The case raises urgent questions about the duty of care owed to incarcerated individuals facing preventable, environment-driven illness.

Editor's Note: Incarcerated populations remain among the most vulnerable and least-heard victims of toxic mold exposure — their health outcomes deserve the same rigorous attention as any other patient population.
