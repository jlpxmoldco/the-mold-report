# People Magazine Covers Reality Star's Battle With Mold-Related Chronic Illness

> Published: 2026-04-24 | Source: People | Category: news

People magazine has spotlighted Mikayla Matthews' ongoing health battle with a chronic illness linked to mold exposure, bringing mainstream media attention to an issue that affects millions of Americans living in water-damaged buildings. Matthews, who appears on Hulu's The Secret Lives of Mormon Wives, has described her condition as having stripped her of happiness, citing skin pain, neurological symptoms, and the challenge of managing motherhood with four children while chronically ill.

The coverage in a publication with People's reach represents a significant moment for mold illness awareness. For years, patients with mold-related symptoms have struggled to be taken seriously by conventional medicine, often receiving fragmented diagnoses that address individual symptoms without identifying the environmental root cause.

## Editor's Note

Research suggests that approximately 24% of the population carries HLA-DR gene variants that may make them more susceptible to developing chronic immune responses from biotoxin exposure. Blood biomarker testing — including TGF-β1, MMP-9, and MSH — can help identify whether environmental exposure is driving symptoms.
