# Midcoast School Faces $4M Bill to Address Black Mold Contamination

> Published: 2026-03-27 | Source: Bangor Daily News | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/b8ea489c6a33.html
Original source: https://www.bangordailynews.com/2026/03/27/midcoast/midcoast-health/black-mold-nobleboro-school/

---

A midcoast school is confronting a potentially massive remediation bill after black mold was discovered on the property, with preliminary estimates putting the cleanup cost at around $4 million, according to local news reports. While the specific location has not been named in this summary, reports indicate the discovery likely followed routine maintenance work or complaints from staff and students about air quality. Mold infestations of this scale in school buildings typically require the removal and replacement of affected building materials, including drywall, insulation, and flooring, along with upgrades to ventilation systems to prevent recurrence. School officials are likely weighing options that include partial remediation, full closure during cleanup, or in extreme cases, a full facility rebuild. The financial burden raises questions about how the district will fund the project, whether through local budgets, state assistance, or federal grants. Mold exposure in school environments may pose health risks to children and staff, particularly those with respiratory conditions or genetic susceptibilities to mold-related illness. The situation underscores the ongoing challenge many aging school buildings face with moisture control and indoor air quality.
