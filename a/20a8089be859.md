# ANU Student Housing Found Contaminated With Toxic Black Mold

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: The Canberra Times | Author: canberratimes.com.au | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/20a8089be859.html
Original source: https://www.canberratimes.com.au/story/8552395/anu-x-unilodge-lena-karmel-lodge-filled-with-toxic-black-mould-student/

---

A former student has filed suit against the Australian National University following her stay at Lena Karmel Lodge, alleging that prolonged mold exposure contributed to health concerns. The case highlights ongoing concerns about indoor air quality standards and institutional accountability in student housing. Research suggests some individuals report health symptoms they associate with mold exposure. Residents concerned about mold in university or shared housing should consult with healthcare providers and document their living conditions. This account has not been independently verified by The Mold Report.

## Editor's Note

CIRS litigation against housing providers is an emerging area of law that may set important precedents for tenant protections in mold-contaminated environments.
