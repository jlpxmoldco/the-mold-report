# Mold in Australian Homes Linked to Health and Social Problems

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: PubMed / Public Health Res Pract | Category: research

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/4977bfb7b6f7.html
Original source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40479519/

---

An Australian qualitative study examined the health and social consequences experienced by residents living in mold-affected homes, finding that exposure extends well beyond physical symptoms to affect daily functioning and quality of life. Researchers Gatto, Li, and Bentley documented how mold-compromised housing creates compounding burdens for occupants, underscoring the need for housing policy and clinical practice to treat mold exposure as a serious public health concern.

## Editor's Note

Published in Public Health Research & Practice, this study adds to a growing body of international evidence that mold in residential settings carries meaningful human costs beyond property damage.
