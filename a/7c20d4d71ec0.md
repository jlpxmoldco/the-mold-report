# Chronic Inflammatory Response Syndrome: Evidence for Treatment Efficacy

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: PubMed / Ann Med Surg (Lond) | Category: research

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/7c20d4d71ec0.html
Original source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39649915/

---

A peer-reviewed paper published in Annals of Medicine and Surgery examines research on treatments related to Chronic Inflammatory Response Syndrome (CIRS), a condition studied by researchers in connection with mold and biotoxin exposure. Authors Dooley, Vukelic, and Jim review what current research suggests regarding therapeutic approaches. For readers interested in this emerging area of study, this kind of evidence-based analysis presents how researchers are evaluating various interventions. This account has not been independently verified by The Mold Report.

## Editor's Note

CIRS treatment protocols continue to gain peer-reviewed scrutiny, which is an important step toward broader medical recognition of biotoxin-related illness.
