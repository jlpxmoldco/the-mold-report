# NSW Health Minister Apologizes Over Bird Lice and Mold Outbreaks in Public Hospitals

> Published: 2026-02-03 | Source: 2GB | Author: Local News | Category: regulation

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/6a18e9374816.html
Original source: https://www.2gb.com/bird-lice-maintenance-failures-impacting-nsw-health-facilities/

---

New South Wales Health Minister issued a public apology following reports of bird lice infestations and mold outbreaks discovered in NSW public hospitals, according to local news. The incidents appear to have raised serious concerns about maintenance standards and patient safety across the state's health infrastructure. Reports indicate the outbreaks affected multiple facilities, prompting government accountability questions. Mold in hospital environments poses particular risks, as research suggests exposure may contribute to respiratory issues and other health complications, especially for patients who are already immunocompromised. The minister's apology signals acknowledgment of systemic failures in building upkeep and infection control protocols. Authorities are expected to outline remediation plans, though critics may argue the response came too slowly. The dual nature of the problem, combining pest infestation with mold contamination, points to broader infrastructure neglect in public health facilities. NSW hospital administrators are likely under pressure to conduct audits of other facilities to determine whether similar conditions exist elsewhere in the network.
