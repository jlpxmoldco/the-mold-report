# Indoor Mold and Mycotoxins: Health Risks Explained

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: Scholar / probiologists.com | Category: research

A 2025 review published in the Archives of Clinical Toxicology examines the specific molds, mycotoxins, and bacteria most commonly found in buildings and most relevant to occupant health. The authors identify the primary fungal species and their toxic byproducts that clinicians and building investigators should understand when assessing indoor exposure risk. This peer-reviewed work provides a structured reference for evaluating mold-related health concerns in residential and commercial environments.

## Editor's Note

Peer-reviewed toxicology literature on mycotoxin exposure remains an important foundation for informed clinical and remediation decisions.
