# Parents and Students Protest Mold Conditions at Chatsworth Park Elementary

> Published: 2026-03-12 | Source: ABC7 Los Angeles | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/16962afb59bd.html
Original source: https://abc7.com/post/students-parents-hold-protest-moldy-conditions-chatsworth-park-elementary-school/18704145/

---

Families and students at Chatsworth Park Elementary School staged a public protest over what they describe as unacceptable mold conditions inside the building. Reports indicate that parents have grown frustrated with the pace of the school's response to visible mold growth, prompting them to take their concerns outside school grounds. Mold in school buildings is a recurring issue across the country, particularly in older structures where water intrusion and poor ventilation create conditions favorable to mold growth. Children may be especially vulnerable to mold exposure, as research suggests it can trigger respiratory symptoms, allergic reactions, and other health effects. When schools fail to address contamination quickly, community members often escalate pressure through organized demonstrations. It is not yet clear whether the district has committed to remediation or a formal inspection timeline. Local news coverage of the protest suggests the community wants concrete action, not just acknowledgment of the problem. The situation at Chatsworth Park reflects a broader pattern of deferred maintenance in public school facilities leaving students and staff exposed.
