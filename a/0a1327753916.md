# Oxford parents demand transparency after mold found at Quaker Farms School

> Published: 2026-04-26 | Source: MSN / WTNH | Author: WTNH News 8 | Category: news

---

Parents at Quaker Farms School in Oxford, Connecticut are pressing district leaders for clearer, faster communication after mold was discovered inside the building. Several families say their children have reported dizziness, headaches, and stomach pain that they attribute to the indoor exposure, and they want to know exactly where the contamination was found, what the air-quality readings looked like, and when remediation will be complete.

The complaint pattern lines up with what families typically describe in water-damaged school buildings: nonspecific symptoms that affect a cluster of students in the same wing or classroom, often appearing after rain events or HVAC issues. Parents at the meeting asked for routine moisture mapping, third-party air testing, and a written remediation plan rather than ad-hoc updates.

District officials said work is underway. Parents requested a parent-facing channel for ongoing updates and said they want a clear definition of "all clear" before students return to affected rooms.

## Editor's Note

School mold cases are a common entry point into mold illness for kids. The symptoms parents are describing -- headaches, dizziness, GI complaints -- are consistent with the inflammatory response Dr. Ritchie Shoemaker has documented in genetically susceptible patients (roughly 24% of the population) exposed to water-damaged buildings. Air sampling alone often misses the problem; ERMI dust testing and visible-moisture inspections give a fuller picture.
