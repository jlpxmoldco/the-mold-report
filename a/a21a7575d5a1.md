# Louisiana Ranked Most At-Risk State for Mold Growth in New Analysis

> Published: 2026-01-07 | Source: American Home Shield | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/a21a7575d5a1.html
Original source: https://www.ahs.com/home-matters/lifestyle/moldiest-homes-in-america-ranking/

---

Louisiana has been identified as the highest-risk state for mold growth in the United States, according to a recent analysis. The ranking likely reflects the state's combination of high humidity, frequent rainfall, warm temperatures, and vulnerability to flooding — conditions that create near-ideal environments for mold proliferation year-round. Louisiana's Gulf Coast location and history of major storm events, including hurricanes that cause widespread water damage to homes and buildings, further compound the risk. Reports indicate the findings draw on climate and environmental data to rank states by mold growth potential. For residents, the implications are significant. Mold exposure in homes and workplaces may contribute to respiratory issues, allergic reactions, and other health concerns. Research suggests that a subset of the population carries a genetic susceptibility, estimated at around 24%, that may make them more vulnerable to mold-related illness. Experts generally recommend regular home inspections, prompt water damage remediation, and proper ventilation as key preventive measures. The ranking serves as a reminder that in high-humidity states like Louisiana, mold awareness is a year-round public health consideration.
