# Birmingham Public Housing Resident Reports Rats, Mice, and Mold in Home

> Published: 2026-01-06 | Source: CBS 42 | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/6468cf60fc95.html
Original source: https://www.cbs42.com/news/your-voice-your-station/birmingham-public-housing-resident-says-home-full-of-rats-mice-and-mold/

---

A Birmingham public housing resident has come forward with complaints about severe living conditions, including infestations of rats and mice alongside significant mold growth inside their unit. Reports indicate the resident has raised concerns with housing authorities about the uninhabitable state of the property. Cases like this are common in older public housing stock, where moisture intrusion, poor ventilation, and deferred maintenance create conditions that allow mold to take hold and spread. Mold growth in residential settings can pose serious health risks, particularly for children, the elderly, and individuals with respiratory conditions or immune system vulnerabilities. Research suggests that prolonged exposure to indoor mold may contribute to respiratory symptoms, chronic fatigue, and other health issues. The complaint puts a spotlight on the broader challenge facing public housing agencies across the country, many of which struggle with aging infrastructure and limited maintenance budgets. Local advocates and housing officials have not yet issued a formal public response, according to available reports.
