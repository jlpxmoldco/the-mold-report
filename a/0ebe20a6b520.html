Woman with serious illness denied release from prison, advocates urge Whitmer

Source: www.google.com 2026-04-17 2

Krystal Clark, a 41-year-old mother of four incarcerated at Women's Huron Valley Correctional Facility, is facing a serious illness that some advocates have attributed to mold exposure within the facility. Advocates are urging Governor Whitmer to grant her compassionate release. This account has not been independently verified by The Mold Report. The case highlights concerns some have raised about mold in correctional facilities and inmate health protections. Note: The Mold Report does not diagnose, treat, or confirm mold-related illnesses.

Editor's Note: Mold exposure in institutional settings carries well-documented health consequences, and incarcerated individuals remain among the most vulnerable and least-heard populations affected.
