Mold Volatile Compounds Trigger Toxicity in Office Building

Source: Scholar / probiologists.com 2026-04-18 2

A peer-reviewed case report documents a 43-year-old man with health symptoms following mold exposure in an office building. The case suggests volatile organic compounds emitted by mold may warrant investigation alongside more commonly studied exposures like spores, hyphal fragments, or mycotoxins. Research exploring mold-related health impacts continues to examine multiple exposure pathways. This account has not been independently verified by The Mold Report.
