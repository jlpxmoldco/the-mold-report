# Disney Cruise Line Guest Reports Mold on Room-Service Food After Disney Adventure Cancellation

> Published: 2026-05-11 | Source: Inside the Magic | Author: Andrew Boardwine | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/b3e08f17ca59.html
Original source: https://insidethemagic.net/2026/05/after-abrupt-cancellations-mold-and-rust-uncovered-on-disney-cruise-line-ab1/

---

A guest aboard the Disney Adventure — Disney Cruise Line's newest vessel, sailing out of Singapore — reported finding visible mold on a cheesecake delivered through room service, according to a Inside the Magic report citing firsthand social-media accounts. The guest said they documented the dessert with photographs and notified onboard staff, who offered chocolate-dipped strawberries as a goodwill gesture.

The incident surfaced shortly after Disney abruptly canceled a four-night Disney Adventure sailing in Singapore over what the company described as an unresolved mechanical issue, leaving boarded guests scrambling to rearrange travel plans. Affected passengers received refunds and travel assistance. There is no indication from the report that the mold finding was part of a broader sanitation failure.

Cruise vessels operate under strict sanitation regulations because of how readily illness can spread in shared dining and ventilation systems. Visible mold on prepared food, when documented, typically triggers internal food-safety reviews regardless of how isolated the incident appears.
