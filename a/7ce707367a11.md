# Jaipur Doctor Creates Diagnostic Tool for Hypersensitivity Pneumonitis

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: ETV Bharat | Author: etvbharat.com | Category: news

Original source: https://www.etvbharat.com/en/health/dr-sheetu-singh-a-respiratory-disease-specialist-from-jaipur-has-developed-a-new-tool-for-the-identification-of-hypersensitivity-pneumonitis-enn26041802596

---

A physician in Jaipur has developed a structured diagnostic tool designed to identify Hypersensitivity Pneumonitis, a lung inflammation condition triggered by repeated exposure to organic antigens such as mold, bird droppings, or animal dander. The tool, which focuses on systematically evaluating a patient's exposure history, has received recognition from pulmonology experts internationally. For patients experiencing unexplained respiratory symptoms, this kind of standardized approach to diagnosis can be a meaningful step toward identifying an environmental cause and beginning appropriate treatment.

## Editor's Note

Hypersensitivity Pneumonitis is frequently misdiagnosed; tools that center environmental exposure history are a welcome development for both clinicians and patients seeking answers.
