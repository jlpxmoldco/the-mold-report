Hospitals Combat Mold Infestations to Protect Patient Safety

Source: PubMed / GMS Hyg Infect Control 2026-04-18 2 min read

A peer-reviewed paper in GMS Hygiene and Infection Control examines how hospitals and medical facilities identify and manage mold infestations, with a focus on protecting vulnerable patients from fungal exposure risks. The authors outline practical approaches to remediation and prevention within clinical environments where immunocompromised individuals face the greatest harm.

Editor's Note: Mold management in healthcare settings carries higher stakes than in residential or commercial buildings — infection risk for at-risk patients makes prompt, methodical response essential.
