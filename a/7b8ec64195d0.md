# Oxford Parents Demand Better Communication After Mold Found at Elementary School

> Published: 2026-03-25 | Source: WTNH | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/7b8ec64195d0.html
Original source: https://www.wtnh.com/news/connecticut/new-haven/oxford-parents-ask-for-increased-communication-after-mold-found-at-quaker-farms-school/

---

Parents in Oxford are pressing school officials for greater transparency after mold was discovered at Quaker Farms School, reports indicate. The discovery prompted concerns about student and staff health, with families calling for clearer updates on remediation timelines, testing results, and any potential health risks linked to the mold presence. School mold incidents often trigger a breakdown in trust between administrators and families, particularly when communication is delayed or vague. In cases like this, districts typically face pressure to share air quality test results, outline remediation steps, and clarify when affected areas will be safe for occupancy. Local news coverage suggests parents learned of the mold through unofficial channels before receiving formal notice from the school, which fueled frustration. Mold exposure in school buildings is a recognized public health concern, as children may be more vulnerable to airborne mold spores than adults. Research suggests that prolonged exposure in poorly ventilated indoor environments may contribute to respiratory symptoms and other mold-related illness. Officials have not yet confirmed the extent of the contamination or a remediation completion date.
