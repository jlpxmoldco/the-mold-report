# Garibaldi City Hall Shut Down Indefinitely Over Mold Concerns

> Published: 2026-03-03 | Source: Tillamook Headlight Herald | Author: Local News | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/21bbaa20e44d.html
Original source: https://www.tillamookheadlightherald.com/news/garibaldi-city-hall-closed-indefinitely-amid-mold-concerns/article_9983c3ff-d70f-48c4-ad8b-04ca8caf0dac.html

---

Garibaldi City Hall has been closed indefinitely after mold was discovered in the building, according to local reports. Officials have not announced a reopening timeline, suggesting the contamination may be more extensive than a routine remediation job. Reports indicate the closure was triggered by visible mold growth or air quality concerns serious enough to prompt an emergency shutdown rather than a scheduled inspection response. City staff are likely working from temporary locations while the building undergoes assessment. Mold closures of public buildings often follow complaints from employees about respiratory symptoms or other health issues, though the specific circumstances here have not been confirmed. The closure raises questions about maintenance history and whether the building had prior water intrusion issues that went unaddressed. Municipal buildings of a certain age are particularly vulnerable to mold growth due to aging infrastructure and HVAC systems. Residents and city employees will be watching for updates on remediation timelines and cost estimates, both of which can run high when contamination is found throughout a structure.
