# Inhaled Black Mold Toxicity Study in Mice Reveals Health Effects

> Published: 2026-04-18 | Source: PubMed / Toxic Rep Ser | Category: research

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/7b761cfcb022.html
Original source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39441587/

---

A National Toxicology Program study examined the health effects of inhaled Stachybotrys chartarum — commonly known as black mold — in a mouse model. Researchers administered the mold by inhalation to B6C3F1/N mice and documented the resulting toxic responses. The findings contribute to the foundational science needed to better understand human health risks from mold exposure.

## Editor's Note

Stachybotrys chartarum is among the most clinically significant indoor molds and has been a focus of occupant health concerns for decades.
