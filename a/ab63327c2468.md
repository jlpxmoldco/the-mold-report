# Wisconsin Doctor Warns Flood Victims: Mold Growth Begins Within Days

> Published: 2026-04-24 | Source: WTAQ | Category: news

Canonical URL: https://themoldreport.org/a/ab63327c2468.html
Original source: https://wtaq.com/2026/04/23/thedacare-doctor-warns-of-mold-damage-in-homes-after-floods-urges-health-and-safety-steps/

---

Dr. William Kumprey, an emergency medicine physician and associate chief medical officer for ThedaCare, is warning Northeast Wisconsin residents affected by April 2026 flooding that mold can develop in water-damaged homes within two to three days — with respiratory symptoms potentially appearing days, weeks, or even months after exposure begins.

Kumprey advises that homeowners can self-remediate small mold areas under 10 square feet within 24-48 hours, but larger affected areas involving carpeting and drywall require professional remediation. He cautioned that continued exposure in enclosed environments compounds health risks including respiratory illness and asthma.

## Editor's Note

Post-flood mold growth is one of the most common sources of biotoxin exposure in residential settings. Research suggests that approximately 50% of residential buildings have some degree of water damage. For individuals with genetic susceptibility, even short-term exposure to mold in water-damaged buildings may trigger chronic inflammatory responses that persist long after the initial exposure.
